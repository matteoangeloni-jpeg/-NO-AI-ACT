# Legal Disclaimer — NO AI ACT

NO AI ACT is an **educational serious game** about the European Artificial
Intelligence Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689, the "AI Act"). This document states
clearly what it is — and what it is not.

## Not legal advice

NO AI ACT is a **didactic simulation**. It is **not legal advice**, not a legal
opinion, and not a substitute for:

- the official text of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 and its annexes;
- national implementing measures and guidance;
- the advice of a qualified legal professional.

Nothing in the game or its documentation should be relied upon to determine the
legal status, classification or compliance of any real artificial intelligence
system.

## Simplified by design

To be teachable, the game **deliberately simplifies** the regulation. Risk
categories, obligations, roles and outcomes are streamlined into a four-level
result (compliant / partial / contestable / non-compliant). Real cases are more
detailed, more contextual and may turn on facts the game does not model.

## Cases are fictional

All cases, organisations, documents and "evidence" are **fictional** and created
for teaching. Any resemblance to real systems, bodies or events is coincidental.
The cases are framed by **context of use**, not as blanket statements that a kind
of system (e.g. a public chatbot, a generative model, an EdTech platform, or a
procurement process) is always prohibited or always high-risk.

## No professional relationship

Using NO AI ACT does not create any advisory, consulting or professional
relationship. The authors and contributors make **no warranty** as to accuracy,
completeness or fitness for any particular purpose, and accept **no liability**
for decisions taken on the basis of the game.

## For institutional use

Before any formal institutional, public-sector or commercial use — for example
training that informs real compliance decisions — a **third-party legal review**
is recommended, alongside the actual regulatory text.

## Privacy

NO AI ACT collects **no personal data**. There is no backend and no account. The
public pages use aggregate, privacy-friendly Cloudflare Web Analytics (no cookies,
no personal data) to measure page usage; the game itself sends no analytics,
results, answers or reports, and progress is stored only in the browser's local
storage. See the project brief and the on-site privacy section for detail.
